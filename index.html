Welcome to the Fanny Barks Wellie Wangers sports club!

Welcome to the Fanny Barks Wellie Wangers sports club!

We have been wanging wellies weekly for well over half a century. Every week we meet to practise wanging and get in shape for the calendar year.

We have been national and international champions on the Wellie Wanging circuit, winning the world championships in Upperthong in 2008, 2012 and 2014.

Wellie wanging is fabled to have begun in Upperthong after a collision between two farmers in a pub. One spilt beer in the other's wellie and in the ensuing fracas, the wet-socked victim removed his wellie and began clobbering his clumsy foe with it. When the beer spiller ran off, the offended party threw his wellie at him.

But we work hard and play hard and usually finish up from our practise sessions with a pint down the pub - served in a glass, not a wellie!
